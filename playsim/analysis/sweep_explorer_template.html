Illustrative synthetic data — not a validated retention claim
Each cell is a deterministic run of the closed-loop room simulator over a shared, seeded, policy-independent arrival stream (the A/B invariant). “Win” = the candidate policy beats standard on the chosen metric within that seed; stability counts how many of the seeds agree. Throughput (total seat-hrs) structurally rewards concentration; vulnerable seat-hrs is the FairPlay cohort check. Numbers are illustrative until calibrated to real room traffic.
